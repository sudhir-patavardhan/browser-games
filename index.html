BROWSER GAMES

A collection of single-file browser games. No installs, no build step — just open and play.

Chroma Blocks

A radiant, neon falling-blocks game in the spirit of Tetris — hold queue, ghost piece, combo effects, and procedural audio.

Last 16

An arcade football game set at the World Cup 2026 Round of 16 — pick a real nation and star player, control them directly, and fight through a real bracket to the final.

Break Room

A physics-driven 8-ball pool game — full rules with fouls and ball-in-hand, spin control, an AI opponent with three difficulties, and local 2-player pass-and-play.

Daśānana

A Rāmāyaṇa astra-duel — counter Rāvaṇa's divine missiles with the true response, chant the Āditya-Hṛdayam to restore your tejas, and end the ten-headed king with the Brahmāstra.

Fairway Four

A full-3D golf course in a single file — four authored holes, a three-click swing meter, real ball flight with wind, draw and fade, cinematic camera swoops, and a sloped-green putting grid.

Ennead

Configurable tic-tac-toe — play Classic on any board from 3×3 up to 9×9 gomoku, or Ultimate: nine nested sub-boards where each move sends your opponent onward. Local 2-player or a three-level AI.
