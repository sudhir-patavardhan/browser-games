DRIFT

SLIDE · CHAIN · SEND IT

An endless sunlit road that keeps throwing tighter corners. Pin the throttle down the straights, brake into the bends — carry too much speed and the rear steps out. Hold the slide to build your multiplier; the wider and longer, the bigger the score.

Keyboard: ← → (or A / D) steer  ·  ↑ / W throttle  ·  ↓ / S brake
Touch: hold the left / right side to steer — or swipe to turn the wheel in driver view. The car drives itself; a second finger anywhere is the brake
To slide: brake as you turn in — the tail steps out. Then get back on the power to hold it
Cruise: the left thumb pad on the wheel (or C) — it holds your speed, but the corners are still yours. Braking cancels it. The right pad skips a track
Stereo: tap the dash screen — art plays/pauses, middle skips, meter mutes  ·  keys K / N / P / M
Views: you drive from behind the wheel. Tap “Top view” (or press V) for a look down at the corner coming — the dash also has an AR guide and a nav map
