DRIFT

SLIDE · CHAIN · SEND IT

An endless sunlit road that keeps throwing tighter corners. Pin the throttle down the straights, brake into the bends — carry too much speed and the rear steps out. Hold the slide to build your multiplier; the wider and longer, the bigger the score.

Keyboard: ← → (or A / D) steer  ·  ↑ / W throttle  ·  ↓ / S brake
Touch: hold the left / right side to steer — or swipe to turn the wheel in driver view. The car drives itself; a second finger anywhere is the brake
To slide: brake as you turn in — the tail steps out. Then get back on the power to hold it
Cruise: the left thumb pad on the wheel (or C) — it holds your speed, but the corners are still yours. Braking cancels it. The right pad skips a track
Stereo: tap the dash screen — art plays/pauses, middle skips, meter mutes  ·  keys K / N / P / M
Views: you drive from behind the wheel. Tap “Top view” (or press V) for a look down at the corner coming — the dash also has an AR guide and a nav map
Contracts: the county posts three jobs every run — top-left, under your wallet. Cross one mid-run and the cash lands on the spot
Today’s Road: one road per day — same corners, same horde, same jobs for everyone. Its today’s best resets at midnight, and it never touches your endless BEST. Your best run comes back as a ghost — race the phantom, watch the ±m gap. Some days it rains: grip and brakes are down, and the whole county drives the same weather
Zombies: the horde has ranks — runners sprint for your lane and pay double, brutes soak two rounds, pay triple, and cost you real speed if you plow one. Every couple of klicks the radio calls a BOUNTY WAVE — a dense pack, double pay for 18s, and five heads inside the window clears it for a +$120 bonus. The road has a horde problem and the county pays a bounty — run them down for wallet cash (it persists). The wallet buys guns above; an equipped gun auto-fires at the shamblers out on the verge that the car can’t reach. Brushing past one at speed without touching it is a CLOSE SHAVE — it feeds your multiplier and keeps a drift chain alive between corners
Garage: bigger money buys car hardware — a longer-range pack, a regen tune, track tyres. Fitted once, driven on every run after
Trophies: firsts and feats land on the shelf above, for good — and driving Today’s Road day after day builds a streak the shelf pays out on
